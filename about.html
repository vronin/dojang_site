World Tang Soo Do Association

The World Tang Soo Do Association (WTSDA) is an international organization dedicated to preserving traditional Korean martial arts and promoting the values of respect, discipline, and self-improvement.

What is Tang Soo Do?

Tang Soo Do is a Korean martial art that blends elements of traditional Karate and Chinese Kung Fu. It emphasizes striking, kicking, and self-defense techniques, along with a strong focus on character development and inner strength.
